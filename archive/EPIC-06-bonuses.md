# EPIC-06 Bonuses (Instant & Consumable)

## Goal
Add instant and consumable bonuses with activation rules.

## Scope
- Instant bonuses: Hail of Shapes, Color Grenade
- Consumables: Touch to Kill, Machine Gun
- Cooldowns, session limits
- Integration with scoring and chains

## Out of scope
- Shop pricing and upgrades

## Deliverables
- Bonus system state
- Activation and effects
- UI hooks (minimal)
