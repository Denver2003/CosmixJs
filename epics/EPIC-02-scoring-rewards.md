# EPIC-02 Scoring & Rewards

## Goal
Implement score and coin rewards for chain collapses and level milestones.

## Scope
- Score formula (NR)
- Bubble score formula (PR)
- Level-up bonus (NLR)
- Money formula (MR)
- Persist score/coins per session (record-only for score)

## Out of scope
- Combo system
- Cosmometer
- Bubbles spawn logic
- Shop/progression

## Deliverables
- Score/coin calculations
- Reward application on collapse
- Level-up bonus payouts
- Display hooks for score/coins
